Company Knowledge Base

Welcome to the internal knowledge base of the company. This document contains basic policies, guidelines, and frequently asked information.

Working Hours:
Employees are required to work from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, Monday to Friday. Flexible timing may be allowed depending on department approval.

Leave Policy:
Each employee is entitled to 24 paid leaves per year. Sick leave and casual leave are included in this quota. Leave must be approved by the manager before taking time off.

Remote Work Policy:
Employees may work remotely up to 2 days per week if approved by their team lead. Some roles may require full-time office attendance.

Probation Period:
All new employees undergo a probation period of 3 months. Performance is reviewed at the end of this period before confirmation.

Salary Payments:
Salaries are paid on the 1st of every month. If the 1st falls on a holiday, payment will be made on the previous working day.

Code of Conduct:
Employees must maintain professionalism, respect colleagues, and follow company ethics. Any violation may result in disciplinary action.

IT Support:
For any technical issues, employees should contact the IT support team via email or internal ticketing system.

Data Security:
Company data must not be shared outside the organization without proper authorization. Employees are responsible for maintaining confidentiality.

Training and Development:
The company provides regular training sessions to improve employee skills. Participation in selected training programs may be mandatory.

End of Document.
